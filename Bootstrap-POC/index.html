Crusine is an online delivery service where users can log-in and shop groceries from partner stores in their local area, dropped off directly at their door.

It's as simple as filling your cart, picking a time and place, and paying for what you need. We take care of the rest.

Select your favorite store or browse for the groceries you need. Once you check out, our employees work behind the scenes to shop for your items, package them, and transport them at the time window requested. This eliminates time, resources, and energy you would need to plan and use for each trip to the grocery store. Now at your fingertips, you can get the groceries you want and need at your convenience from the safety of your campus!
